WHO ARE WE?

The story of ‘’Ceramico’’ begins in 2014, from this small clay workshop, which was
located in just 170 square meters and had a collective of five people. Despite many
difficulties that have arisen many times over the years, as a result of the great efforts
and selfless work of each employee, multi-faceted ceramic wine bottles were created,
and over a decade, the foundation was laid for the construction of our own enterprise,
where today the best team is gathered around the beloved work.
Its founder and CEO, an advertising specialist by profession, could never have
imagined that he would start doing something completely different in this field.
“At certain moments, I thought that I would not be able to cope with the work of this
scale, but working with clay taught me to never give up and to fight to the end for what
is important to me. Moreover, from today’s point of view, I think that this is the job that I
love very much and it works best for me” he says.
The variety and depth of clay proved very attractive, with its challenges and difficulties.
Being an ancient and very interesting field in its origins, clay has endured decades and
has preserved its uniqueness for many centuries.
The demand for clay products is still relevant today in many areas. Clay pots have a
special place in Georgian winemaking.

Our production is staffed with the best team, which allows us to produce quality
products. Over ten years we have made many friends as loyal customers. We are proud
to cooperate with wine companies that are popular not only in Georgia, but also outside
the borders of the country. We are glad that our products are competitive and occupy a
place on store shelves in different countries of the world.
In addition to the drinkware, since 2020 we have added new directions, we have started
working on white clay and we are making beautiful products, we are working on
individual orders.
We also prepare a variety of flower pots with different clay. We beautify markets all over
Georgia with our pots. In this business, the most interesting and fascinating thing is to
interact with new and interesting people.
I think the best people are gathered in our team. Clay has amazing power, it
strengthens you, it charges you, and the work process is unified and harmonious. Clay
taught us to take care of each other and we became one family. We study together, train
each other and support each other.
I try to create as free and friendly atmosphere as possible in our small family, our
relationship is built on mutual respect. A thing created with love carries a completely
different power. We are happy with the fact that with our noble work we make a small
but useful contribution to the development of Georgian production.
